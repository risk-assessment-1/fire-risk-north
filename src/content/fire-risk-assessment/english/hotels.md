## Fire Risk Assessment for Hotels

Hotels, guest houses, and bed and breakfasts present unique fire safety challenges because guests are sleeping in unfamiliar surroundings and may be disoriented during a night-time evacuation. The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 places specific duties on hotel operators to ensure that fire detection, escape routes, and evacuation procedures are designed to protect sleeping occupants at all hours.

### Key Considerations

- Guest room fire detection and early warning
- Corridor escape route protection and lighting
- Night-time evacuation procedures and staffing
- Kitchen and laundry fire risks

### What We Assess

- Fire detection and warning systems

- Fire fighting equipment
