## Fire Risk Assessment for High-Rise Buildings

High-rise buildings over 18 metres present unique fire safety challenges that require specialist assessment. Following the Grenfell Tower fire and subsequent regulatory changes, high-rise buildings face heightened scrutiny around external wall systems, evacuation strategies, and firefighting access. Our assessors are fully trained in the latest post-Grenfell requirements and legislation.

### Key Considerations

- Evacuation strategy (stay put vs simultaneous)
- Firefighting access and lift provision
- Cladding and external wall assessment
- Smoke control systems and ventilation

### What We Assess

- Means of escape routes and signage
- Fire detection and warning systems
- Emergency lighting
- Fire fighting equipment
- Fire safety management procedures
- External wall fire review (post-Grenfell requirements)
