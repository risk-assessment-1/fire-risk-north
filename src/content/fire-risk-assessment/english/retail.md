## Fire Risk Assessment for Retail Premises

Retail shops and shopping centres must balance fire safety with open customer access and commercial display requirements. Under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, retailers must ensure that escape routes can handle the maximum expected occupancy, that display materials meet fire retardancy standards, and that staff are trained to manage customer evacuation safely and efficiently.

### Key Considerations

- Public escape route capacity and signage
- Display material fire retardancy compliance
- Stock room fire risk and storage arrangements
- Disabled access evacuation provision

### What We Assess

- Means of escape routes and signage
- Fire detection and warning systems
- Emergency lighting
- Fire fighting equipment
- Fire safety management procedures
- Crowd management and escape capacity calculations
