## Fire Risk Assessment for Schools

Schools and educational establishments have a duty of care to protect large numbers of young people who require supervised evacuation. Fire risk assessments must address the specific risks posed by laboratories, workshops, and kitchens, as well as the challenges of managing evacuation when community groups use the premises outside of school hours. The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 applies alongside Department for Education guidance on fire safety in schools.

### Key Considerations

- Assembly point planning and roll call procedures
- Laboratory and workshop fire risk management
- Young person evacuation procedures and supervision
- Out-of-hours community use fire plans

- Fire detection and warning systems

- Fire fighting equipment

- Supervised evacuation for young persons
