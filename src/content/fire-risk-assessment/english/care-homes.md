## Fire Risk Assessment for Care Homes

Care homes and residential care facilities must have fire risk assessments that account for residents with reduced mobility, cognitive impairments, or medical dependencies that affect their ability to evacuate without assistance. Under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 and CQC registration requirements, care home operators must demonstrate that evacuation plans are tailored to each resident's level of dependency and that staff are trained to execute those plans effectively.

### Key Considerations

- Resident mobility assessment for evacuation planning
- Bedroom fire safety and detection
- Staff training and fire drill frequency
- Oxygen and medical equipment storage
