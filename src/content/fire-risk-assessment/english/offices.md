## Fire Risk Assessment for Offices

Office buildings and workplaces must have a current fire risk assessment under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005. Employers and building managers are responsible for ensuring that all staff, visitors, and contractors can evacuate safely in the event of a fire. Modern open-plan offices and flexible working spaces present specific challenges for escape route design and fire detection.

### Key Considerations

- Staff fire marshal provision and training
- Open-plan escape route design and signage
- Electrical equipment fire risk in server and comms rooms
- Disabled persons evacuation plans

### What We Assess

- Means of escape routes and signage
- Fire detection and warning systems
- Emergency lighting
- Fire fighting equipment
- Fire safety management procedures
- PEEP (Personal Emergency Evacuation Plans) for employees
