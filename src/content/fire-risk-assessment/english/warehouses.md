## Fire Risk Assessment for Warehouses

Warehouses and industrial units carry substantial fire risk due to high fire loading from stored goods, the presence of hazardous materials, and large open floor areas that can allow rapid fire spread. The Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005 requires a thorough fire risk assessment that accounts for storage arrangements, material types, and the specific challenges of evacuating personnel from large industrial spaces.

### Key Considerations

- Storage arrangements and fire loading
- Hazardous materials storage and segregation
- Large area escape route planning
- Sprinkler and suppression systems

### What We Assess

- Means of escape routes and signage
- Fire detection and warning systems
- Emergency lighting
- Fire fighting equipment
- Fire safety management procedures
- High-bay storage fire risk assessment
