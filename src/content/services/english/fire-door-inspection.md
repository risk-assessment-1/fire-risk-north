## Fire Door Inspection

Regular fire door inspections are essential to maintain the integrity of your building's fire compartmentation. Our qualified inspectors assess each door against current standards and regulations.

### What's Included

- Visual and physical inspection of all fire door assemblies
- Certification and labelling verification
- Gap measurement and assessment
- Closer and hardware functionality testing
- Smoke seal and intumescent strip checks
- Detailed inspection report with pass/fail classification

### Regulatory Requirements

Under the Fire Safety Order 2005, responsible persons must ensure that fire doors are subject to a suitable system of maintenance. Regular inspections help demonstrate compliance and ensure occupant safety.
