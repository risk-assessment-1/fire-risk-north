## Fire Extinguisher Servicing

Fire extinguishers must be serviced annually to comply with BS 5306-3. Our qualified technicians inspect, test and maintain your extinguishers to ensure they are ready for use when needed.

### What's Included

- Visual inspection of all extinguishers
- Weight and pressure checks
- Discharge test (where required)
- Replacement of seals and tamper indicators
- Labelling and signage verification
- Service certificate and log book update

### Types We Service

- Water extinguishers
- Foam extinguishers
- CO2 extinguishers
- Dry powder extinguishers
- Wet chemical extinguishers
- Fire blankets
