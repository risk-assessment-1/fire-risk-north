## Asbestos Surveys

Asbestos remains present in many buildings constructed before 2000. Our asbestos surveys identify and assess the risk of asbestos-containing materials (ACMs) in your premises, helping you comply with the Control of Asbestos Regulations 2012.

### What's Included

- Thorough site inspection and sampling
- Laboratory analysis of suspect materials
- Comprehensive asbestos register
- Risk assessment for each identified ACM
- Management plan recommendations
- Photographic documentation

### Types of Surveys

- **Management Survey:** Standard survey for ongoing management of ACMs in occupied buildings
- **Refurbishment/Demolition Survey:** Required before any major renovation or demolition work
