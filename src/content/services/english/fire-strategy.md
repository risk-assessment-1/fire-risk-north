## Fire Strategy Development

A fire strategy is a comprehensive document that outlines how a building is designed and managed to ensure the safety of its occupants in the event of a fire. Our fire strategies are developed in accordance with Approved Document B and BS 9999.

### What's Included

- Means of warning and escape
- Internal fire spread (linings and structure)
- External fire spread
- Access and facilities for the fire service
- Fire safety management procedures
- Evacuation strategy

### When You Need a Fire Strategy

- New build developments
- Change of use applications
- Building regulation approvals
- Major refurbishments
- Complex or high-rise buildings
