## Fire Alarm Testing

Your fire alarm system is the first line of defence in alerting occupants to a fire. Our fire alarm testing service ensures your system is fully functional and compliant with BS 5839-1.

### What's Included

- Full system test of all detectors and call points
- Control panel inspection and testing
- Sounder level testing
- Battery backup verification
- Log book review and update
- Certificate of testing

### Testing Schedule

- **Weekly:** Manual call point testing (rotated)
- **Monthly:** Generator/battery backup tests
- **Quarterly:** Quarterly service visits
- **Six-monthly:** Periodic inspection and testing
- **Annual:** Full system inspection and test
