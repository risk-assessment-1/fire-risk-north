## Emergency Light Testing

Emergency lighting is essential for safe evacuation during a power failure or emergency. Our testing service ensures your emergency lighting system complies with BS 5266-1 and operates correctly when needed.

### What's Included

- Full functional test of all emergency luminaires
- Duration test (3-hour discharge test)
- Illumination level assessment
- Battery condition check
- Wiring and connection inspection
- Test certificate and log book update

### Testing Schedule

- **Monthly:** Brief functional test (simulate mains failure)
- **Annual:** Full duration test (3-hour simulated mains failure)
- **Ongoing:** Visual inspections as part of routine checks
