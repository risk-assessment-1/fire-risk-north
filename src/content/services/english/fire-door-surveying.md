## Fire Door Surveying

Fire doors are a critical component of any building's fire safety strategy. Our fire door surveying service ensures that all fire doors on your premises are correctly installed, maintained and fit for purpose.

### What's Included

- Comprehensive inspection of all fire doors
- Assessment of door condition, gaps and seals
- Hardware compatibility checks (hinges, locks, closers)
- Intumescent strip and smoke seal inspection
- Photographic evidence and detailed reporting
- Recommendations for remedial work

### Why Fire Door Surveying Matters

Faulty fire doors are one of the most common fire safety deficiencies found during inspections. A properly functioning fire door can contain a fire for 30-60 minutes, giving occupants crucial time to escape.
