## Professional Fire Risk Assessment

Our fire risk assessments are conducted by qualified assessors in full compliance with the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005. We follow the PAS 79 methodology to ensure a thorough and systematic evaluation of your premises.

### What's Included

- Full site inspection and evaluation
- Identification of fire hazards and at-risk persons
- Assessment of existing fire safety measures
- Detailed written report with actionable recommendations
- Compliance certification

### Who Needs a Fire Risk Assessment?

All non-domestic premises in the UK are legally required to have a suitable and sufficient fire risk assessment. This includes:

- Commercial offices and retail premises
- Residential blocks of flats (common areas)
- Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs)
- Hotels and guest houses
- Schools and educational establishments
- Healthcare facilities and care homes
- Restaurants, pubs and cafes
- Warehouses and industrial units
