What is a fire risk assessment? +

A fire risk assessment is a systematic evaluation of your premises to identify potential fire hazards, people at risk, and measures needed to reduce or remove fire risks. It’s a legal requirement under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005.

Who needs a fire risk assessment? +

Any business, landlord, or person responsible for a building must conduct a fire risk assessment. This includes offices, shops, warehouses, HMOs, care homes, schools, and any premises where people work or visit.

How long does an assessment take? +

Most assessments take 1-3 hours depending on property size and complexity. We offer 24-hour report turnaround, with same-day service available for urgent requirements.

How much does it cost? +

Our fire risk assessments are at the best market rates, significantly lower than competitors who charge £245+. Final pricing depends on property size, type, and complexity. Get an instant quote using our calculator.

What’s included in the report? +

Our comprehensive report includes hazard identification, people at risk assessment, existing fire safety measures evaluation, prioritized action plan, and recommendations for improvements to ensure full compliance.

How often should I update my assessment? +

Fire risk assessments should be reviewed annually or whenever significant changes occur to the building, its use, or occupancy. We offer annual review packages to ensure ongoing compliance.
